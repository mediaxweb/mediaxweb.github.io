Job description:

MediaX is Vietnam's leading provider of AI solutions. We develop AI products and services that empower organizations and enterprises to leverage automation, data analytics, and intelligent virtual assistants

  • Support design & development of AI agents for businesses (Content Engine, Chatbot, Review Treatment)
  • Build workflow automation for quick demo phase (7-10 days)
  • Support design & implementation of AI agents (tooling, function-calling, short-term memory, basic RAG).
  • Build and operate workflow using n8n (queue, retry, pagination).
  • Integration with popular APIs: Gmail, Google Sheets/Drive, Telegram/Slack, Meta Graph, Web Scraper.

Your skills and experience:

  • Basic knowledge of Node.js or Python.
  • Understands how to work with APIs (REST, Webhooks).

  • Meticulous, eager to learn, organized thinking, and clear communication.

Why you'll love working here?

  • Receive direct training & mentorship from the MediaX AI development team.
