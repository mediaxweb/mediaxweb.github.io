About MediaX:

MediaX is an innovative AI startup based in Vietnam. We are passionate about leveraging AI and machine learning to drive business transformation and create cutting-edge digital solutions. As a growing company, we offer a unique opportunity to be part of a collaborative team where you can make a real impact, learn quickly, and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of technology. Join us on the journey of shaping the future of AI!

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with senior developers and data scientists to design, develop, and deploy machine learning models tailored to client needs.
  • Engage in data collection, cleaning, preprocessing, and feature engineering to ensure data is ready for model training.
  • Develop and optimize machine learning algorithms, focusing on efficiency, scalability, and accuracy.
  • Test, validate, and fine-tune models to meet performance criteria.
  • Integrate machine learning models into MediaX’s digital solutions and client projects.
  • Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI and machine learning, and contribute to continuous improvement.
  • Assist in the documentation of machine learning processes and workflows.

Qualifications:

  • A degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Solid understanding of machine learning concepts, algorithms, and best practices.
  • Proficiency in Python, with experience using machine learning libraries such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-learn.
  • Familiarity with data handling and preprocessing techniques.
  • Basic knowledge of SQL and database management.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to learn and adapt quickly.
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to work effectively in a team.

Nice to Have:

  • Experience with cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure.
  • Familiarity with big data frameworks such as Hadoop or Spark.
  • Understanding of deep learning and neural networks.
  • Experience using version control systems like Git.
  • Prior experience with AI-driven digital solutions or projects.

Why Join MediaX?

  • Be part of a growing team at the forefront of AI and digital transformation.
  • Gain hands-on experience in cutting-edge machine learning projects.
  • Opportunities for rapid learning and career growth in a startup environment.
  • Flexible work conditions and the opportunity to directly impact business projects.
  • Potential for equity or stock options.
