Full-stack Engineer (TypeScript & NextJS)

About Us

Mediak helps Singapore and US tech companies build and manage software engineering teams in Vietnam. We are currently developing our own product, an AI-powered online coding test platform that helps HR personnel assess technical capabilities of software engineering candidates quickly, easily, and accurately. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in developing an AI application and fine-tuning AI models, such as LIMS and vision models.

Responsibilities

  • Daily tasks may include but are not limited to: maintaining and fixing bugs in existing features.
  • Participating in the end-to-end development process, including frontend (NextJS/ReactJS), backend development (NestJS/TypeScript) and testing.
  • Troubleshooting and debugging issues, identifying bottlenecks, and proposing solutions to optimize application performance.

Requirements

  • Must-Have: At least a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Software Engineering.
  • Prior coding experience (any language will do).
  • Strong algorithmic thinking skills.

Preferred:

  • Prior coding experience in frontend (ReactJS & NextJS) or backend development (NodeJS & TypeScript).

Culture Fit:

  • Self-learner: We provide resources and guidance, but you must take the initiative to self-learn whatever is required for your job as we experiment with new and untested campaigns.

Benefits:

  • Performance review every 6 months.
  • Opportunities to build an AI application and fine-tune AI models.

Interview Process:

  • 1 written technical test.
  • 1 interview.
